Overview

About SA

A professional qualification designed to develop highly skilled accounting, finance, taxation, auditing, corporate governance, and business professionals.

Core Disciplines

  • Accounting
  • Finance
  • Taxation
  • Auditing
  • Business Mgmt
  • Corporate Law
  • Info Systems
  • Data Analytics
  • AI in Finance
  • Risk Mgmt
  • Ethics
  • Leadership

Our Vision

To develop globally competent accounting and finance professionals who uphold integrity, innovation, and excellence.

Our Mission

  • Build strong accounting fundamentals.
  • Develop analytical decision-making skills.
  • Promote ethical professional conduct.
  • Strengthen business leadership.
  • Prepare for global finance careers.
  • Encourage digital transformation.

Program Structure

The qualification consists of three progressive levels, followed by practical training.

Eligibility

  • Class XII (PUC or equivalent), or
  • Undergraduate Degree, or
  • Equivalent qualification recognized by SIPA.

Qualification Levels

Level I: SA Foundation

Purpose: To develop strong fundamentals in commerce, accounting, economics, quantitative aptitude, and business communication.

  • Principles of Accounting
  • Business Economics
  • Business Mathematics & Statistics
  • Business Law
  • Commercial Communication
  • Fundamentals of Finance
  • Information Technology Basics
  • Professional Ethics

Level II: SA Intermediate

Purpose: To build advanced professional knowledge in finance, accounting, taxation, auditing, and corporate law.

  • Financial Accounting
  • Cost Accounting
  • Management Accounting
  • Corporate Accounting
  • Financial Management
  • Direct Taxation
  • Indirect Taxation
  • Corporate Laws
  • Auditing & Assurance
  • Business Information Systems
  • Financial Analytics

Level III: SA Professional

Purpose: To prepare candidates for senior leadership and specialist professional roles.

  • Advanced Financial Reporting
  • Strategic Financial Management
  • International Taxation
  • Advanced Auditing
  • Corporate Governance
  • Risk Management
  • Investment Analysis
  • Financial Strategy
  • Business Valuation
  • AI in Finance
  • Data Analytics
  • Ethics & Professional Standards

Training & Examination

The program integrates theoretical knowledge with practical training to prepare candidates for careers in diverse sectors.

Practical Training

Candidates must complete professional practical training under approved organizations.

  • Financial Accounting
  • Internal Audit
  • Statutory Audit
  • Tax Compliance
  • Corporate Finance
  • Payroll Management
  • Financial Reporting
  • ERP Systems
  • Data Analytics
  • Business Consulting

Examination System

  • Evaluation Areas: Conceptual understanding, Practical application, Analytical reasoning, Financial decision making, Ethical judgment, Professional competence.
  • Question Types: Multiple Choice, Numerical Problems, Practical Accounting Entries, Ledger Preparation, Case Studies, Tax Computation, Audit Planning, Data Interpretation.
  • Marking Scheme: Full marks for correct answers, negative marking for incorrect objective answers, step-wise marking for descriptive questions, method/accuracy evaluation for practical problems.
  • Examination Modes: Computer Based Examination, Descriptive Papers, Practical Assessments, Case Study Evaluation.

Certification

Successful candidates receive the certification:

Surya Accountant (SA)

Members may use the professional designation SA.
Example: Rahul Sharma, SA
