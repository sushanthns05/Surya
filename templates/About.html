About Surya Group of Industries

An overview of our mission, values and role in education.

Who we are

Surya Group of Industries established the Surya Education Board (SEB) as an autonomous testing organisation to deliver reliable, transparent and internationally aligned assessments.

Our Mission

To improve equity and quality in education by developing research-based, valid, and secure assessments that support student success and institutional decisions.

What we do

  • Design and administer standardized entrance tests
  • Publish specimen and past papers for student preparation
  • Engage teachers, parents, and institutions to improve assessment quality

History & Governance

SEB has conducted examinations on behalf of Surya Group of Industries since 2001, operating under the Societies Registration Act and guided by best practices in assessment security and fairness.
