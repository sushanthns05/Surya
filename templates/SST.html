Overview

About SST

A national-level scholarship examination designed to identify academically talented students and provide financial assistance, fee waivers, merit awards, and academic opportunities.

Core Evaluations

  • Conceptual Understanding
  • Analytical Ability
  • Logical Thinking
  • Scientific Reasoning
  • Quantitative Aptitude
  • Problem Solving

Why Choose SST

  • National Recognition
  • Merit Scholarships
  • Transparent Selection
  • Online Examination
  • AI Proctoring
  • Digital Certificates

Examination Cycles

Conducted twice every year in Computer-Based Test (CBT) and Offline modes.

  • Spring Session: January–March
  • Autumn Session: August–October

Scholarship Categories

SST is open to students from school, pre-university, and higher education. Choose the category that applies to your academic level.

SST-J: Junior

  • Class IX
  • Class X

SST-P: Pre-University

  • 1st PUC
  • 2nd PUC

SST-U: Undergraduate

  • B.Tech (1st - 4th Year)

SST-G: Graduate

  • M.Tech (1st - 2nd Year)
