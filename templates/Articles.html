Official Exam Articles

Mandatory exam regulations for Surya Education Board candidates and participants.

Article 1

All candidates must carry a valid photo ID and the official admit card at the exam venue.

Article 2

No electronic devices, smart watches, calculators, or unauthorized materials are allowed inside the exam hall.

Article 3

Any attempt to cheat, copy, or use unfair means will be treated as a serious violation.

Article 4

Candidates must follow the instructions of invigilators and exam staff at all times.

Article 5

Talking, communicating, or sharing information with other candidates during the exam is strictly prohibited.

Article 6

Leaving the exam room without permission is not allowed; exit only with invigilator approval.

Article 7

All exam materials must be handled respectfully and returned when instructed.

Article 8

Use of abusive language, disrespectful behavior, or disruptive conduct is forbidden.

Article 9

Candidates must arrive on time. Late entry may be denied and may risk invalidation.

Article 10

Do not bring banned items such as weapons, tobacco, or other prohibited objects to the exam venue.

Article 11

Any suspected violation must be reported immediately to exam authorities or invigilators.

Article 12

Breaking any one of these articles will result in debarment and cancellation of exam records under Surya Education Board regulations.
