    Exam Details

    ParameterDetails
    ExaminationSurya Admission Test [SAT]
    Conducting BodySurya Education Board (SEB)
    Duration3 Hours (180 Minutes)

    MediumEnglish / Kannada

    Paper Structure — Each Subject (30 Questions)

    SectionTypeQuestionsCorrectWrongUnattempted
    Section AMultiple Choice (Single Correct)20+4 Marks−1 Mark0

    * This is a fixed paper structure. Every subject (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics) follows the same 20 MCQ + 10 Numerical pattern.

    Question Status — Colour Legend

    1

    Important Rules

    • Do NOT switch browser tabs or minimize the window. Each violation is logged.
    • Right-click, copy-paste, and keyboard shortcuts (Ctrl+C, F12, etc.) are strictly disabled.
    • The exam runs in fullscreen mode. Exiting fullscreen gives 3 warnings; the 4th fullscreen exit will auto-submit the exam.
    • Click Save & Next to record your answer before proceeding.
    • Mark for Review & Next saves the answer (if given) and flags the question for review.
    • You may change your answer any number of times before final submission.
    • The exam will auto-submit when the timer reaches 00:00:00.
    • For Integer Type (Section B), use the on-screen numpad to enter a numerical value.
    • Not Visited questions are NOT evaluated.
    • After clicking Submit, you cannot return to the exam.
    • More than 3 tab switches will result in automatic submission.
