Overview

About KSPUEAB

Responsible for higher secondary education, preparing students for university studies, professional careers, research, entrepreneurship, and global opportunities.

The KSPUEAB Approach

A balanced approach to theory, laboratory work, projects, and skill development:

  • Conceptual learning & Scientific thinking
  • Analytical reasoning & Mathematical rigor
  • Research-based education & Innovation
  • Technology integration & Leadership

Our Vision

To nurture academically strong, innovative, ethical, and globally competent learners prepared for higher education, research, entrepreneurship, and professional excellence.

Official Motto

Learn • Research • Innovate • Excel

Our Mission

  • Build strong conceptual foundations.
  • Foster innovation and scientific inquiry.
  • Develop research aptitude.
  • Integrate technology into education.
  • Prepare students for international opportunities.

PUC Structure & Streams

The two-year Pre-University Course is designed for competitive examination readiness and university preparation.

1st PUC Focus

  • Introducing advanced subjects
  • Laboratory learning & Skill development

2nd PUC Focus

  • Advanced concepts & problem solving
  • Research-oriented learning
  • Competitive examination readiness

Academic Deep Dive

Science Stream Focus Areas

Physics

Mechanics, Thermodynamics, Waves, Oscillations, Electricity, Magnetism, Optics, Modern Physics.

Includes laboratory experiments, numerical analysis, graph interpretation, and real-world applications.

Chemistry

Physical, Organic, Inorganic Chemistry, Electrochemistry, Kinetics, Biomolecules.

Laboratory work includes qualitative analysis, volumetric analysis, and experimental techniques.

Mathematics

Algebra, Trigonometry, Calculus, Coordinate Geometry, Probability, Differential Equations.

Assessment emphasizes proofs, logical reasoning, modelling, and advanced problem solving.

Computer Science & Biology

CS: Programming, SQL, Cyber Security, AI Fundamentals, Algorithms.
Bio: Genetics, Human Physiology, Biotechnology.

Practical work focuses on coding and databases (CS) and scientific investigation (Bio).

Assessment Model & Typology

Student performance is comprehensively evaluated through Unit Tests, Mid-Term Examination, Practical Examination, Internal Assessment, and the Final Board Examination.

Internal & Practical Assessment

  • Laboratory Records
  • Experimental Analysis
  • Observation Skills
  • Project Demonstration
  • Viva Voce
  • Research Activities
  • Seminars
  • Classroom Participation
