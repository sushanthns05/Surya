About SME

The SURYA MEDICAL EXAM (SME) is a prestigious national-level standardized medical entrance examination conducted by the SURYA Examination Authority (SEA) for admission into undergraduate and postgraduate medical and allied health science programs offered by SURYA Universities, SURYA Medical Colleges, and affiliated institutions.

SME is designed to assess a candidate's academic knowledge, scientific aptitude, analytical reasoning, ethical decision-making, and readiness for medical education. The examination follows a transparent, merit-based admission system inspired by internationally accepted testing standards.

Vision

To identify, nurture, and empower future healthcare professionals by conducting a transparent, fair, innovative, and merit-based medical entrance examination that upholds the highest standards of academic excellence and integrity.

Mission

  • Promote equal educational opportunities for all students.
  • Conduct secure and transparent medical entrance examinations.
  • Encourage conceptual understanding rather than rote memorization.
  • Select deserving candidates purely on merit.
  • Support talented students through scholarships.
  • Develop future doctors, nurses, pharmacists, researchers, and healthcare leaders.

Objectives

  • Evaluate scientific knowledge and conceptual understanding.
  • Measure analytical thinking and logical reasoning.
  • Assess aptitude for medical education.
  • Ensure a transparent and standardized admission process.
  • Provide a single national entrance examination for medical admissions.
  • Identify academically outstanding students for scholarships.
  • Maintain high standards in healthcare education.

Governing Authority (SEA)

SURYA Examination Authority (SEA)

An autonomous examination body responsible for:

  • Conducting SME examinations.
  • Developing examination policies.
  • Preparing question papers.
  • Maintaining examination security.
  • Publishing answer keys.
  • Declaring results.
  • Conducting counselling.
  • Allocating seats.
  • Awarding scholarships.
  • Maintaining academic integrity.

Examination Levels

SME-UG

Eligibility:

Completed Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology, and English.

Purpose:

Admission into Undergraduate Medical Programs.

SME-PG

Eligibility:

MBBS or equivalent recognized qualification.

Purpose:

Admission into Postgraduate Medical Programs.

SME-SUPER SPECIALITY

Eligibility:

MD/MS/DNB or equivalent.

Purpose:

Admission into Super Speciality Medical Courses.

Courses Covered

MBBS BDS BAMS BHMS BUMS BSMS BPT B.Sc Nursing Post Basic Nursing GNM Pharmacy (B.Pharm, D.Pharm) Medical Laboratory Technology Radiology Operation Theatre Technology Emergency Medicine Dialysis Technology Optometry Audiology Respiratory Therapy Cardiac Care Technology Public Health Biomedical Sciences Nutrition & Dietetics Clinical Research Paramedical Programs Postgraduate Medical Courses Super Speciality Courses
