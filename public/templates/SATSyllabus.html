SAT 2027 Test Papers & Syllabus

Official Subject Syllabuses for SURYA ADMISSION TEST

SAT 2027 will be conducted for 30 test papers. A candidate is allowed to appear either in ONE or UP TO TWO test papers. Each SAT paper is for a total of 100 marks, General Aptitude (GA) is common for all papers (15 marks), and the rest of the paper covers the respective test paper syllabus (85 marks).
