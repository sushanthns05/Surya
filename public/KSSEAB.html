The Apex Educational Authority

Karnataka Surya School Education and Assessment Board (KSSEAB)

The autonomous authority regulating, developing, and administering secondary and higher secondary education across affiliated institutions.

"Educating Minds • Inspiring Innovation • Building the Future"

Overview

About KSSEAB

Providing a unified educational framework that promotes academic excellence, innovation, research, ethics, digital learning, and holistic student development.

Our Vision

To establish a world-class education system that empowers learners through knowledge, innovation, ethics, research, creativity, and technology while preparing them for global careers.

Core Values

  • Excellence
  • Integrity
  • Innovation
  • Accountability
  • Inclusiveness
  • Transparency
  • Leadership

Our Mission

  • Deliver high-quality education.
  • Build strong conceptual understanding.
  • Encourage scientific inquiry.
  • Promote innovation and entrepreneurship.
  • Integrate technology into learning.

Organizational Structure
